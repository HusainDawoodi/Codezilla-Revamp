FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is backend development, and why does my business need it?

Backend development involves building the server-side logic and database of a web application. Your business needs it to handle data, manage users, and ensure your platform works correctly and securely behind the scenes.

Which technologies do you use for backend development?

We use a variety of modern technologies like Node.js, Python, and Ruby on Rails. We choose the best tools based on your project's specific needs to ensure high performance and scalability.

How do you ensure the security of the backend?

We implement strong security measures like data encryption, secure authentication, and regular updates. Our team follows industry best practices to protect your data and prevent unauthorized access.

Can you help migrate my existing backend to the cloud?

Yes, we specialize in migrating backends to cloud services like AWS and Azure. We ensure a smooth transition with minimal downtime, improving your platform's reliability and scalability.

How long does it take to develop a custom backend?

The timeline depends on the complexity of your project and the features you need. After discussing your requirements, we provide a clear plan and estimate to keep everything on schedule.
